Reflections

The fonts I paired together in this assignment are Roboto and Roboto Slab, as they are both from the same font family, and thus compliment each other.

In addition to the four colors from my color palette that I generated from huemint.com, I added a red and a green color for the CTA buttons in the cards, as it is often by convention that green signifies accept/go and red signifies cancel/stop, i.e from traffic lights in real life.

In retrospect, I could have used the colors from my generated color palette instead to emphasize one button over the other, by making the other button a ghost button variant.
